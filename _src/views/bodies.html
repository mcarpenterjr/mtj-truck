Van Bodies

We offer Van Bodies built by Hewitt Lucas, who have been in business since 1960, and offer and extremely durable Van Body that like our other installed bodies will out last the chassis it's been built on. Currently we maintain a fleet of over 30 Hewitt Lucas bodies that age in range from twenty five years to a year old.

Each body is built to order by Hewitt Lucas, installed and finished by M.T.J.

Options

Each Van body is customizable, allowing you to choose door configurations, bumper style, lighting options and color. Ease loading with a liftgate, ramp or dock bumper with integrated steps. Add a side door for ease of access, choose from half, two thirds, or a single roll up door for rear access.

Need a specific color to match your existing fleet or company's logo? Not a problem, each body can be skinned to your desired color or finished off in a mirrored stainless. We can even work with your current advertising agent and meet your fleet billboard needs.

The body interior can be furnished with shelves or a load handling system if needed. Integrating E-track into the side panels as well as integrated J-track in the floor and ceiling.
