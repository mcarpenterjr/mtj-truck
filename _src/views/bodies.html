Van Bodies

We offer Van Bodies built by Hewitt Lucas, who have been in business since 1960, and offer and extremely durable Van Body that like our other installed bodies will out last the chassis it's been built on. Currently we maintain a fleet of over 30 Hewitt Lucas bodies that age in range from twenty five years to a year old.

Each body is built to order by Hewitt Lucas, installed and finished by M.T.J.
